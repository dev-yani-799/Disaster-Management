Disaster Information

Disasters in Indian States

Disaster States Affected Damage How to Act
Floods Assam, Bihar, Kerala, Maharashtra, Uttar Pradesh Property damage, loss of life, displacement Move to higher ground, avoid flooded areas, and listen to local authorities for evacuation orders.
Cyclones Odisha, West Bengal, Andhra Pradesh, Tamil Nadu Wind damage, flooding, power outages Evacuate if advised. Secure loose objects, board up windows, and stay indoors during the storm.
